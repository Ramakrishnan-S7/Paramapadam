The Divine Objective

The ultimate goal is to navigate your token from the earthly realm (Square 1) to the final square (Square 100), which represents Moksha or liberation from the cycle of rebirth.

The Fellowship

Two or more seekers may participate. Traditionally, it is played as a community event during festivals like Vaikuntha Ekadashi to encourage collective spiritual reflection.

Laws of Karma (Movement)

  • The Roll: Movement is governed by a single die (or traditionally, cowrie shells).
  • The Path: Players must follow the numerical sequence strictly, snaking back and forth across the board.

Virtue and Vice

The board is a test of character. Landing on the foot of a Ladder (a virtue) allows you to ascend immediately. However, landing on the head of a Snake (a vice) causes an immediate descent to its tail.
